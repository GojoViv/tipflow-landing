How It Works

Scan to bank.
In under 30 seconds.

01

Scan or Tap

Customer scans the waiter's QR code on a table card, receipt, or NFC badge. No app install. Opens instantly in mobile browser.

02

Choose & Pay

Select €1 / €2 / €5 or enter a custom amount. Pay via card (Stripe), PayPal, or crypto. Branded confirmation screen on completion.

03

Instant Notification

Staff receive a push notification instantly via their PWA. Tip accumulates in their TipFlow balance with full gross/net breakdown.

04

SEPA Payout

Tips paid out to staff bank accounts daily or weekly via SEPA Instant or SEPA Credit Transfer. Automated reconciliation.

The Platform

Every component.
Production-grade from day one.

Component What It Does
QR Tipping Web App
Mobile-optimised web page per staff member. Preset + custom tip amounts. Card (Stripe), PayPal, crypto payments. Branded confirmation screen. No app download required.
Payment Engine
Stripe for cards, PayPal SDK, Coinbase Commerce for crypto. Webhook-driven status tracking with idempotent processing. Multi-currency support: EUR, PLN, CZK, RON, HUF, BGN.
Staff PWA
Progressive Web App with real-time tip feed, balance, payout history, push notifications. Installable from browser — no app store. Works offline.
Payout Engine
SEPA Instant and SEPA Credit Transfer via Stripe Connect. Configurable daily/weekly schedule. Automated reconciliation. Full gross/fee/net transparency on every payout.
Business Dashboard
Venue and staff management, QR code generation and assignment, tip analytics with date filtering, payout controls. Multi-site support built in from day one.
Tip Pooling
Optional venue-level pooling. Split by equal share or hours worked. Foundation for advanced allocation rules and future AI-driven fairness scoring.
Audit Trail
Immutable, append-only transaction log for every tip, allocation, and payout. AES-256 encrypted at rest. GDPR-compliant. Designed for future local tax authority integration per jurisdiction.

PCI compliance via Stripe — no raw card data
