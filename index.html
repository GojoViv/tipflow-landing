What Gets Built

Everything the platform needs.

QR Tipping Web App

Mobile-optimised page per staff member. Preset amounts (€1/€2/€5) or custom. No app install required. Branded confirmation screen.

Payment Engine

Stripe, PayPal SDK, Coinbase Commerce. Webhook-driven, idempotent processing. Multi-currency: EUR, PLN, CZK, RON, HUF, BGN.

Staff PWA

Progressive Web App: real-time tip feed, balance, payout history, push notifications. Installable from browser — no app store needed.

Payout Engine

SEPA Instant & Credit Transfer via Stripe Connect. Daily or weekly schedule. Automated reconciliation. Gross/fee/net transparency for every payout.

Business Dashboard

Venue & staff management, QR generation, tip analytics with date filtering, payout controls. Multi-site support built in.

Tip Pooling

Optional venue-level pooling. Split by equal share or hours worked. Foundation for advanced allocation rules across teams.

Audit & Compliance Trail
Immutable, append-only transaction log for every tip, allocation, and payout. AES-256 encrypted at rest. GDPR-compliant. Designed for future local tax authority integration per jurisdiction.
