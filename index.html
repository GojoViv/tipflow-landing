How It Works

Set up in an afternoon.
Tips start flowing that evening.

STEP 01

Create your account

Sign up, add your venue details, and connect your bank account. Takes under 5 minutes. We verify your details securely via Stripe.

STEP 02

Add your staff

Add waiters, bartenders, or anyone you want to tip. Each person gets a unique QR code — printed on cards, receipts, or an NFC badge.

STEP 03

Place the QR codes

Put QR cards on tables, add to receipts, or hand out NFC badges. Guests scan with their phone camera — no app needed — and tip in seconds.

STEP 04

Tips flow to the bank

Staff get notified instantly when they receive a tip. Funds land in their bank account daily or weekly via SEPA — with a full breakdown of every payout.
